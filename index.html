About Me

Welcome to my place ! Here you can find more information about me and my projects. I’m François, a 24 years old programmer and student in France.

I started programing when I realized I could not only play games but make ones. Since then I’ve been writing code for all sorts of applications, you can find some examples in the projects section below. I mostly create games and 3D related stuff and i'm particularly interested in computer graphics and engine programming. Right now I’m making a home brew game engine called FEngine in C++ using the Vulkan API.

Don’t hesitate to contact me if you have any question!
